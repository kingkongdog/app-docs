# Privacy Policy

This application (Mouse Wheel Scroll Volume) respects user privacy and fully complies with Apple's privacy requirements.

## 1. Information Collection
This app **does not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

## 2. User Data
The app only modifies system volume locally and does not access or transmit any user personal data.

## 3. Permissions
The app may require access to system volume control and modifier key functions, but all operations are local and no data is sent to external servers.

## 4. Data Sharing
This app **does not share user data with any third party**.
